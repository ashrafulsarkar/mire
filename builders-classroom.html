Michigan Builders License - 60 Hour Class

Classroom Overview

Our Builder classroom courses are taught in a traditional classroom setting with an instructor leading the classes. Our instructors are licensed Builders and offer a wealth of information to aid in your success. Each classroom course includes Passing the Michigan Builders Exam Digital Audio CD set and a Builders Math Digital DVD that will assist when studying for the exam. This is a popular option for someone who works best in a hands-on environment with other students.

Builder 1 Topics

  • Regulatory & Statutory Requirements
  • Marketing & Sales
  • Business Management
  • Estimating Costs
  • Project Management & Scheduling
  • Contracts
  • Risk Management
  • Liablity

Builder 2 Topics

  • Michigan Occupational Safety & House Act (MIOSHA)
  • Michigan Residential Code
  • Building Science
